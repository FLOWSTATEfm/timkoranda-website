About

Tim Koranda Los Angeles
Producer DJ Tech House Techno Flowstate Records

Tim Koranda is a Los Angeles-based producer and DJ crafting House x Techno built for the dance floor, a natural extension of a lifetime spent at the intersection of technology and creative craft.

Since 2011, Tim has built his name across Southern California's most respected rooms: Avalon, Vanguard (now Academy LA), Exchange, The Yost Theater, Sutra (now Time Nightclub), Ten Nightclub in Newport Beach, and a 15-year residency at Brussels Bistro in Laguna Beach — home to the FRQNCY Presents underground — one of the region's defining underground venues, and the famed Monday Night Social in Los Angeles. Beyond SoCal, with appearances at Circuit in Phoenix, MNTRA's Techno Taco Tuesdays in Las Vegas, and Camboriú, Brazil. Rooted in the underground and built on the dancefloor.
