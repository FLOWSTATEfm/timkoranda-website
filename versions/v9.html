About

Tim Koranda Los Angeles
Producer Producer Tech House Techno Flowstate Records

Tim Koranda is a Los Angeles-based producer and DJ crafting deep, soulful House x Techno built for the dancefloor, a natural extension of a decorated career at the intersection of technology and creative craft.

Since 2011, Tim has built his name across Southern California's most respected rooms: Avalon, Vanguard (now Academy LA), Exchange, The Yost Theater, Sutra (now Time Nightclub), Ten Nightclub in Newport Beach, and a 15-year residency at Brussels Bistro in Laguna Beach, one of the region's defining underground venues. Beyond SoCal, with appearances at Monday Night Social in Los Angeles, Circuit in Phoenix, MNTRA's Techno Taco Tuesdays in Las Vegas, and Camboriú, Brazil. Rooted in the underground and built on the dancefloor.
